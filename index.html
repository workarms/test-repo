A little about us

Started in 1995, Lily Academy has evolved into a center of excellence that is not only an academic giant,

but also responsible for producing all rounded students,

who excel always in their chosen fields.

Vision

To be the leading global center for basic education and training.

Mission

To provide early childhood and basic primary education within the 8-4-4 system and new curriculum system.

Over the years, Lily academy has grown and now has two campuses: One in Githurai and the other in Mwihoko.
